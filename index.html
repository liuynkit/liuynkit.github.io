French

French Phoneme-to-Spectrogram Model:

  1. Structure: FastPitch without Pitch Module.
  2. Phoneme Codebook(133): IPA + four french nasal phonemes.
  3. Single-Speaker Model: Pre-trained Models for Spanish + 20,9380 fine-tuned iterations.
  4. Multi-Speaker Model: Single-Speaker Model + 31,149 fine-tuned iterations (still in training).
  5. Dataset: Mailabs. 10,000 sentences(Single-Speaker). 30,000 sentences(Multi-Speaker).

Chinese(Mandarin)

Chinese Phoneme-to-Spectrogram Model:

  1. Structure: FastPitch without Pitch Module.
  2. Phoneme Codebook(147): IPA + 18 chinese special phonemes.
  3. Multi-Speaker Model: Pre-trained Models for French + 172,249 fine-tuned iterations.
  4. Ori. Dataset: Aishell-3.
  5. Training Dataset: Subdataset of 16 North Chinese Males. 10,000 sentences.
