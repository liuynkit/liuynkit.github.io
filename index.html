Spanish

Spanish Phoneme-to-Spectrogram Model:

  1. Structure: FastPitch.
  2. Phoneme Codebook(129): IPA.
  3. Single-Speaker Model: Pre-trained Models for English + 95,000 fine-tuned iterations.
  4. Dataset: Mailabs. Single-speaker. 10,000 sentences.

Vocoder:

  1. Structure: Waveglow vs HiFI-GAN

Metrics:

  1. Realtime-Factor = Processing Duration / Audio Duration
  2. smaller is better

French

French Phoneme-to-Spectrogram Model:

  1. Structure: FastPitch with/without Pitch Module.
  2. Phoneme Codebook(133): IPA + four french nasal phonemes.
  3. Single-Speaker Model: Pre-trained Models for Spanish + 20,9380 fine-tuned iterations.
  4. Dataset: Mailabs. Single-speaker. 10,000 sentences.

Vocoder:

  1. Structure: Waveglow.

Chinese(Mandarin)

Chinese Phoneme-to-Spectrogram Model:

  1. Structure: FastPitch without Pitch Module.
  2. Phoneme Codebook(147): IPA + 18 chinese special phonemes.
  3. Multi-Speaker Model: Pre-trained Models for French + Speaker Embedding layer + 172,249 fine-tuned iterations.
  4. Ori. Dataset: Aishell-3.
  5. Training Dataset: Multi-speaker Subdataset of 16 North Chinese Males. 10,000 sentences.

Vocoder:

  1. Structure: Waveglow.
